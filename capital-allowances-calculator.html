Full expensing vs AIA

Full expensing (introduced April 2023, made permanent from April 2024) gives 100% first-year relief on new qualifying plant and machinery with no monetary limit. AIA also gives 100% but covers second-hand assets and has a £1m annual cap. Either route gives the same result for most small businesses.

Cars are treated differently

Cars cannot use AIA or full expensing. Their treatment depends on CO2 emissions: 0g/km gets 100% FYA; 1-50g/km goes into the main pool at 18% WDA; over 50g/km goes into the special rate pool at 6% WDA.
