What is break-even?

The break-even point is the level of sales at which total revenue equals total costs, resulting in zero profit or loss. Above this point, every unit sold generates profit equal to the contribution margin per unit.

Contribution margin

Contribution per unit = selling price minus variable cost per unit. This is the amount each unit "contributes" towards covering fixed costs and then generating profit. Break-even units = fixed costs divided by contribution per unit.
