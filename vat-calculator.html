A 1% first-year discount applies to businesses in their first year of VAT registration. If your business spends less than 2% of VAT-inclusive turnover (or less than £1,000) on goods, HMRC classes you as a limited cost trader and the 16.5% rate applies instead.

Standard vs Flat Rate

Under the standard scheme you charge 20% VAT and reclaim VAT on purchases. Under the FRS you pay a lower fixed percentage of gross turnover and cannot reclaim input VAT (except on single capital items over £2,000).

VAT threshold 2025

You must register for VAT if your taxable turnover exceeds £90,000 in any rolling 12-month period. You can deregister if it falls below £88,000.
