Penalty reminder

Late filing of annual accounts carries automatic penalties: £150 (up to 1 month late), £375 (1 to 3 months), £750 (3 to 6 months), £1,500 (over 6 months). All penalties double if the company is late two years in a row. Use the late filing penalty calculator to work out your exact exposure.

Key rules

First accounts: due 21 months from incorporation. Subsequent accounts: 9 months from year-end. Confirmation statement: 12 months from last filing, plus 14 days to file. CT payment: 9 months and 1 day from year-end. CT600: 12 months from year-end.

Changing your year-end

You can shorten your accounting period at any time. Lengthening it is limited to once every five years and cannot exceed 18 months total. Any change affects the filing deadline for that period.
