Using your own vehicle

If you use your own vehicle for business, you can claim HMRC approved mileage allowance. For sole traders, this reduces your taxable profit. For limited company directors, the company reimburses you at the HMRC rate and deducts it as a business expense.

Passenger payments

If you carry employees as passengers on business journeys, you can also claim 5p per mile per passenger. This is separate from the main mileage allowance.
