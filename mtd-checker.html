What MTD for Income Tax requires

Under MTD for ITSA, you must use HMRC-compatible software to:

  • Keep digital records of business income and expenses
  • Submit quarterly updates to HMRC (four times a year)
  • Submit a final declaration at the end of the tax year (replacing the Self Assessment return)

Compatible software includes FreeAgent, QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, and others. HMRC maintains a full list of approved software on their website.

Results are for guidance only. MTD rules are subject to change. Always check HMRC guidance directly for the most up-to-date requirements. Not financial or legal advice.
MTD for VAT: key facts

All VAT-registered businesses must use MTD-compatible software to keep digital VAT records and file returns. The quarterly filing deadline is 1 calendar month and 7 days after the VAT period ends.

Quarterly update deadlines

MTD ITSA quarterly updates are due on: 5 August (Q1: 6 Apr to 5 Jul), 5 November (Q2: 6 Jul to 5 Oct), 5 February (Q3: 6 Oct to 5 Jan), 5 May (Q4: 6 Jan to 5 Apr). The final declaration is due by 31 January following the tax year end.
