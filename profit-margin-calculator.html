Gross vs net margin

Gross margin = (Revenue minus COGS) divided by Revenue. Shows how efficiently you deliver your product or service. Net margin = (Revenue minus COGS minus Overheads) divided by Revenue. Shows overall business profitability after all operating costs.

Margin vs markup

Margin is profit as a percentage of selling price. Markup is profit as a percentage of cost. A 50% markup gives a 33.3% margin. A 100% markup gives a 50% margin. They are not interchangeable.
