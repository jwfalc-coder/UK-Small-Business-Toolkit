About UK Small Business Toolkit

Free calculators and tools for UK business owners, built to save you time and help you understand your numbers.

What this site is

UK Small Business Toolkit is a free collection of calculators, trackers, and decision-support tools built for UK limited company directors, sole traders, freelancers, and small business owners.

Running a business in the UK involves a constant stream of tax calculations, compliance deadlines, and financial decisions. We built this toolkit to make those tasks quicker and easier, without requiring an accountancy degree or expensive software.

Every tool on this site is free to use, requires no account or sign-up, and stores nothing on our servers. Your data stays in your browser.

What we cover

The toolkit currently includes 9 tools across 5 categories, with more being added regularly:

  • Company Tax & Finance: Corporation tax calculator with marginal relief, ltd company cost calculator, sole trader vs ltd comparison
  • VAT & Invoicing: VAT calculator with Flat Rate Scheme lookup, professional invoice generator with PDF export
  • Companies House & Compliance: Filing deadline tracker, Making Tax Digital checker
  • Pricing & Profitability: Break-even calculator with profit/loss chart, profit margin calculator with industry benchmarks
Accuracy and rates

All tools are built using current HMRC and Companies House rates and rules. We aim to update them each April when new rates are announced. Key sources include:

  • Corporation tax rates: HMRC guidance
  • Employer NI thresholds: April 2025 changes applied
  • VAT Flat Rate Scheme percentages: current HMRC sector list
  • Companies House filing fees: current published schedule
  • MTD for Income Tax: April 2026 launch and subsequent thresholds
  • National Living Wage: April 2025 rates applied

Despite our best efforts, rates change and edge cases exist. Always verify critical figures with a qualified accountant or directly with HMRC.
