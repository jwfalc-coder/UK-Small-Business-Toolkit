Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

Overview

UK Small Business Toolkit ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

All calculator and tool functionality on this site runs entirely within your browser. Financial figures you enter are not transmitted to, or stored on, our servers.

Information we collect

Calculator inputs: Numbers and dates you enter into our tools are processed locally in your browser and are never sent to us.

Contact form: If you submit a message via our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message in order to respond to you. This data is processed via Formspree and is subject to their privacy policy.

Analytics: We use Google Analytics (GA4) to collect anonymised data about how visitors use this site, including pages viewed, time on site, and approximate geographic location. This data is aggregated and does not identify individuals. It is used to improve the site.

Advertising: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalised advertising via Google Ads Settings.

Cookies

This site uses the following types of cookie:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): Help us understand how visitors use the site. These are only set with your consent.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense): Used to serve relevant advertisements. These are only set with your consent.

You can manage cookie preferences using the consent banner displayed on your first visit, or by adjusting your browser settings.

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics (GA4): Anonymised usage analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense: Advertisement serving. Google Privacy Policy
  • Formspree: Contact form processing. Formspree Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts: Typography. Fonts are loaded from Google servers; your IP address may be logged by Google.
  • jsDelivr CDN: Chart.js library delivery for some pages. jsDelivr Privacy Policy
Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you. As we collect very little personal data, this primarily applies to contact form submissions.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us via the contact form.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be noted at the top of this page with an updated date.
